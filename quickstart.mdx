## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: Project files ready (Markdown, images, or PDFs)

</Callout>

<Callout kind="tip">

Sun 浩 free tier supports up to 5 documentation spaces. Upgrade for unlimited spaces and advanced features.

</Callout>

## Sign Up and Create Your Account

Follow these steps to register and access the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="globe">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.sunhao.com` and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification link from `no-reply@sunhao.com` and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Select your role (Developer, Manager, or Admin) and set your preferences.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Organize your projects by creating dedicated spaces.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Spaces" icon="folder">
    From the dashboard sidebar, click **New Space**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`, add a description, and choose visibility (Private or Public).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Enter" icon="save">
    Click **Create**. You'll land in your new space dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upload Initial Project Files

Add your existing files to the space quickly.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Drag and Drop" icon="upload">
    Open your space, drag files from your desktop into the **Files** section.

    Supported formats: `.md`, `.mdx`, `.png`, `.pdf`, `.zip` (up to 50MB).

    <Image
      src="https://via.placeholder.com/800x400/3B82F6/white?text=Drag+Files+Here"
      alt="Drag and drop files into the upload area"
      width="800"
      height="400"
    />
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="API Upload" icon="code">
    Use the upload API for automation.

    <CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,cURL">
      ```javascript
      const formData = new FormData();
      formData.append('file', fileInput.files[0]);
      formData.append('spaceId', 'your-space-id');

      await fetch('https://api.sunhao.com/v1/files/upload', {
        method: 'POST',
        headers: {
          'Authorization': `Bearer ${YOUR_API_KEY}`
        },
        body: formData
      });
      ```
      ```bash
      curl -X POST https://api.sunhao.com/v1/files/upload \
        -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
        -F "file=@/path/to/project.md" \
        -F "spaceId=your-space-id"
      ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Create Your First Document

Build a simple page to test the editor.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start New Document" icon="plus">
    In your space, click **New Document** and name it `introduction.mdx`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Frontmatter" icon="file-text">
    Insert YAML frontmatter for metadata.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Content" icon="pen-tool">
    Add headings, lists, and components. Preview changes live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="upload-cloud">
    Click **Publish** to make it live. Share the generated URL.
  </Step>
</Steps>
