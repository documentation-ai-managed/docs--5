## Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In">
    Visit https://sunhao.com and sign in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Project">
    Click "New Project" and enter a project name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Documentation">
    Use the "+ New Doc" button to start your first page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize">
    Drag docs into sections for better structure.
  </Step>
</Steps>
